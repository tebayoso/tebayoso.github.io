## Overview

Joined **AgilityFeat** as Lead Software Developer, a software consultancy that grew significantly during my tenure. Grew the engineering team from 5 to 26 developers and led major projects including Decisiv fleet management systems. The company was acquired by Michelin North America in 2018.

## Key Responsibilities

### Team Leadership & Growth
- Grew engineering team from 5 to 26 developers
- Established hiring processes and technical interviews
- Created onboarding programs for new engineers
- Mentored developers and conducted performance reviews

### Technical Leadership
- Led architecture decisions across multiple projects
- Established coding standards and best practices
- Conducted code reviews and technical planning
- Balanced technical debt with delivery velocity

### Client Projects
- Led Decisiv fleet management projects
- Managed multiple concurrent client engagements
- Served as technical liaison with client stakeholders
- Ensured project delivery and client satisfaction

## Decisiv Projects

### Fleet Management Platform
