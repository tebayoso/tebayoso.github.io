## Overview

Founded **Euler Tools**, diving deep into Web3 and blockchain technologies. This entrepreneurial venture allowed me to build decentralized applications and data-driven platforms from the ground up.

## The Journey

### From Idea to Execution
Euler Tools was born from the vision of making blockchain data accessible and actionable:
- Market analysis and validation
- MVP development and iteration
- Team building and scaling
- Product-market fit exploration

## Technical Achievements

### Blockchain Integration
- **Smart Contracts**: Developed and audited Solidity contracts
- **Web3 Integration**: Built bridges between traditional web apps and blockchain
- **DeFi Protocols**: Integrated with various decentralized finance protocols
- **NFT Development**: Created NFT marketplaces and tools

### Full-Stack Development
Built complete platforms including:
- Backend APIs for blockchain data aggregation
- Frontend applications for data visualization
- Real-time price tracking and analytics
- Wallet integrations (MetaMask, WalletConnect)
