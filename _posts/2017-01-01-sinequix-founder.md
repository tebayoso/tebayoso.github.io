## Overview

Founded **Sinequix**, a staff augmentation and consulting company based in Tandil, Argentina. Built the company to provide software development services and consulting to international clients while creating local employment opportunities.

## Company Mission

Sinequix focused on:
- **Staff Augmentation**: Providing skilled developers to international companies
- **Software Consulting**: Technical advisory and implementation services
- **Local Talent Development**: Building tech careers in Tandil, Argentina

## Key Responsibilities

### Business Development
- Established client relationships with US and international companies
- Negotiated contracts and managed project scopes
- Built the company's brand and market presence

### Team Building
- Recruited and hired software developers
- Created onboarding and training programs
- Fostered a culture of continuous learning and excellence

### Technical Leadership
- Architected solutions for client projects
- Conducted code reviews and technical planning
- Mentored team members on best practices

## Services Offered

### Staff Augmentation
- Embedded developers into client teams
- Flexible engagement models (full-time, part-time, project-based)
- Remote-first operations

### Technical Consulting
- Architecture reviews and recommendations
- Technology stack assessments
- Performance optimization

## Technologies

- **Backend**: Ruby on Rails, Node.js, Python
- **Frontend**: React, Angular, Vue.js
- **Mobile**: React Native, Flutter
- **Cloud**: AWS, Google Cloud Platform
- **DevOps**: Docker, Kubernetes, Terraform

## Achievements

- Successfully served multiple international clients
- Created employment opportunities in Tandil's growing tech scene
- Established long-term partnerships with repeat clients
- Maintained high client satisfaction rates

## Overlap with Decisiv

Sinequix operated concurrently with my role at Decisiv (2015-2018). This was a side business that:
- Leveraged my network from consulting work
- Provided additional revenue streams
- Allowed me to help develop local tech talent
- Operated primarily during evenings and weekends

## Learnings

Starting Sinequix taught me:
- **Entrepreneurship**: Building a business from scratch
- **Sales & Marketing**: Finding and closing client deals
- **Financial Management**: Cash flow, invoicing, and profitability
- **People Management**: Leading a distributed team
- **Client Relations**: Managing expectations and delivering value

## Transition

Sinequix was wound down in 2019 as I focused on other opportunities, but the experience informed my later entrepreneurial ventures including Euler Tools and Motosierra Studio.
